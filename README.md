### hi 👋

I'm Juan. I build the plumbing under ML systems — queues, infra, observability, the boring pieces that keep things from breaking at 3 AM.

I love ML infra in general, not just the LLM corner of it. Classical models, GBMs, embeddings, training pipelines, model serving — all of it. The interesting problems usually aren't about the model, they're about the pipeline around it.

I work at [Baubap](https://www.baubap.com) on the **Foundations** team — the platform layer the rest of the company builds on top of.

I take Domain-Driven Design seriously (not just in slide decks), type-checked Python, and infra you can reason about.

<br>

### what i'm up to lately

- **aiokpl** — rewriting AWS's [Kinesis Producer Library](https://github.com/awslabs/amazon-kinesis-producer) (which is C++) in pure-Python async. More out of curiosity than necessity
- **embeddings at scale** — vectorizing large text corpora with Qwen3 on spot GPUs, wrestling with the economics of batching
- **baubap-toolkit** — internal library so teams stop reinventing anyio + spawn-safe multiprocessing every time
- **infra as code** — Pulumi on AWS (Batch, ECR, EKS, observability)
